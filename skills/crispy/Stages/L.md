# Stage L — Learn (Retrospective)

After the task is complete, reflect on the CRISPY run. This feeds the knowledge vault and improves future runs.

## What to produce in `7-L-retrospective.md`

- **What went well** — which artifacts were most useful? where did the process help?
- **What was hard** — where did assumptions miss reality? what surprised you?
- **Lessons learned** — what would you do differently in Q/R/D/S/P next time?
- **Knowledge to share** — anything worth adding to `vault/Shared/Patterns/` or `vault/Shared/Decisions/`?

## Gate

Review with manager or mentor. Extract reusable lessons into `vault/Shared/`.

If a pattern or decision from this task would help the next person facing something similar, write a short note and add it to the shared vault. That's the knowledge contribution social contract.
